FEATURED DESTINATIONS

DARJEELING

DARJEELING

Darjeeling is a city and municipality in the northernmost part of West Bengal, India. It's a popular tourist destination known for its tea industry, views of the Kangchenjunga and Mount Everest.

LADAKH

LADAKH

Ladakh is a cold desert region in the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir.Ladakh is known for its stunning landscapes, clear skies, and high mountain passes. It's also home to Buddhist monasteries, festivals, and adventure activities.

CHILIKA

CHILIKA

Chilika Lake is a brackish water lagoon on India's east coast, in the districts of Puri, Odisha. It is a home to some rare, vulnerable, and endangered species, including dolphins, snakes, turtles, lizards, and around 160 species of fish.

GOA

GOA

Goa is a state on the southwestern coast of India, situated within the Konkan region. Goa is famous for its endless beaches, vibrant nightlife, eclectic seafood, and world-heritage listed architecture.

DWARKA

DWARKA

Dwarka is a town and municipality located in the Devbhumi Dwarka district of the Gujarat state in India. Dwarka is home to the Dwarkadhish Temple, dedicated to Krishna. It is one of the four sacred Hindu pilgrimage sites.

RAMESHWARAM

RAMESHWARAM

Rameswaram is situated on Pamban Island, separated from mainland India by the Pamban channel. According to the Ramayana, Lord Rama built a bridge from this town across the sea to Lanka to rescue his wife Sita from her abductor, Ravana.

KAZIRANGA

KAZIRANGA

Kaziranga National Park is a remarkable wildlife sanctuary located in the Golaghat and Nagaon districts of Assam, India. The park has been recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site due to its exceptional biodiversity.

LAKSHADWEEP

LAKSHADWEEP

Lakshadweep, also known as the Laccadives, is a group of 36 islands situated in the Arabian Sea. The islands have a rich history influenced by various cultures, including the Cheras, Pallavas, and Portuguese.
